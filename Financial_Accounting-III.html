Financial Accounting - III

Ch: 1 Amalgamation of Firms

Meaning

Amalgamation of firms means a closure of two or more business firms and starting a new firm.

Types of Amalgamation

There are two types of Amalgamation:

  • Type 1: Amalgamation in nature of merger.
  • Type 2: Amalgamation in nature of purchase.

Method 1

Amalgamation in nature of purchase â€” there are three steps to follow:

  1. Computation of purchase consideration.
  2. Closure of old books of old firm.
  3. Opening the books of new firm.

Computation of Purchase Consideration

There are two methods to compute purchase consideration:

  1. Lumpsum method: Under this method, the new firm pays the lumpsum amount to the old firm.
  2. Net Asset: Under this method, the purchase consideration is calculated by deducting the amount of liabilities taken over by the new firm from the assets taken over.
