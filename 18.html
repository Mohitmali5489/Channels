General Rules

  • All players should carry their respected ID Cards with them at all times.
  • Teams not arriving/reporting for their match as per scheduled time will forfeit (walkover awarded to opponent).
  • Arguments with the referee/committee members may result in team disqualification.
  • Abusive language is strictly prohibited. Violations result in point deductions.
  • The decision of the organizing committee & referee in all matters is final.

Field Event Rules

Running (100m - 1500m)

  • Sports shoes mandatory. No barefoot running.
  • False start (leaving before whistle): 1 warning, then disqualification.
  • Obstructing another athlete's path leads to disqualification.

Shot Put

  • Enter circle from any direction; exit from the back.
  • Shot must be put from close to neck/chin with a single hand.
  • Shot must land entirely within the sector.

Long Jump

  • Sports shoes mandatory.
  • Take-off must be behind the foul line. Take off from one foot only.
  • Measurement from board to closest mark in sand.
  • Exit sand pit forward. Walking back is a foul.
  • 3 attempts per athlete. Best jump counts.

Team Event Rules

Box Cricket

  • 7 players a side. 4 Overs match.
  • Bowling limit: 1 bowler gets max 2 overs, others 1 over.
  • Hard tennis ball used. Sportswear mandatory.
  • Arguments with umpire = Disqualification.

Tug of War

  • 9 Players per team. 3 Sets.
  • Sports shoes compulsory.
  • NO accessories allowed (Rings, bracelets, kadas, etc).

Relay (4x100m)

  • Team of 4 runners. Baton exchange every 100m.
  • Same running rules (shoes, false starts) apply.

Indoor Rules

Badminton

Non-markable shoes compulsory.
  • Single: 3 sets of 7 points (Semi/Final 11 points).
  • Doubles: Diagonal service. Even score = Right box, Odd score = Left box.
  • Faults: Shuttle lands out, hits net, player touches net, or hits before crossing net.

Chess

  • Time: 5 min per person.
  • Phone vibration = Loss. 2 Illegal moves = Loss.
  • Arbiter decision final.

Carrom

  • 1 Player per team.
  • 3 attempts to break.
  • Foul or no pocket = Turn ends.
