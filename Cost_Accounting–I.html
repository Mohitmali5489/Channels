Module 2: Material Cost

Q2. M/s Reliance Industries Ltd Dhulia supplies you the following information: Consumption of Material per annum: 15,000 Kg Cost of placing an order: Rs. 48 Cost of Raw Material: Rs. 2 per kg Storage cost is 8% of average inventory You are required to ascertain the economic order quantity and also state the number of orders to be placed in the year

Answer:

Annual Consumption = 15,000 kg

Ordering Cost = Rs. 48

Cost per Unit = Rs. 2

Carrying Cost = 8% of Rs. 2 = Rs. 0.16

\( EOQ = \sqrt{\frac{2 \times 15000 \times 48}{0.16}} = \sqrt{\frac{1,440,000}{0.16}} = \sqrt{9,000,000} = \mathbf{3000 \text{ kg}} \)

Number of Orders = \( \frac{15000}{3000} = \mathbf{5 \text{ orders}} \)


3. Calculate economic order quantity from the following: a) Quantity 60,000 units b) Ordering cost Rs. 1,200 per order c) Carrying cost 20% d) Price per unit Rs. 2,000.

Answer:

Annual Demand = 60,000 units

Ordering Cost = Rs. 1,200

Carrying Cost = 20% of Rs. 2000 = Rs. 400

\( EOQ = \sqrt{\frac{2 \times 60000 \times 1200}{400}} = \sqrt{\frac{144000000}{400}} = \sqrt{360000} = \mathbf{600 \text{ units}} \)


Q4. Homework:

Annual Requirement = 1,600 units

Ordering Cost = Rs. 50

Cost per Unit = Rs. 40

Carrying Cost = 10% of Rs. 40 = Rs. 4

Answer:

\( EOQ = \sqrt{\frac{2 \times 1600 \times 50}{4}} = \sqrt{\frac{160000}{4}} = \sqrt{40000} = \mathbf{200 \text{ units}} \)


Q5. Homework:
